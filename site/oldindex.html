What Lorebox does

A complete, local-first toolkit for collectors.

🔎

Smart identification

Scan a card or import an image and Lorebox reads the name, set, number, year, and game automatically.

📊

Condition grading

An automated visual inspection estimates each card's condition — corners, edges, surface, centering.

💰

Market value estimates

Live pricing from official sources (Scryfall for Magic, eBay for the rest), adjusted for condition.

📦

Batch & PDF import

Import whole folders or PDFs, pair fronts and backs automatically, and even auto-import on a schedule.

📈

Dashboard & reports

See total value, value over time, breakdowns by game and grade, and set-completion progress.

🔒

Private by design

No accounts, no tracking, no cloud. Your collection lives in a local database on your PC.

Estimates, not guarantees. Values and condition grades are automated estimates to help you organize and explore your collection — they are not guaranteed sale prices, professional appraisals, or a substitute for a professional grading service.
