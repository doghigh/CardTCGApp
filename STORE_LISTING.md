## Description  (paste as plain text)
Lorebox turns a shoebox of cards into an organized, valued collection — and it
does it privately, right on your computer.

Scan cards with a TWAIN scanner or import images and PDFs, and Lorebox
automatically reads each card's name, set, number, year, and game. It estimates
the card's condition, looks up an approximate market value, and files it into a
searchable collection with dashboards, reports, and set-completion tracking.

Built for collectors of sports cards and trading card games alike.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Identify cards automatically from a scan or photo
• Get an estimated condition grade for every card
• See approximate market values, adjusted for condition
• Browse, search, and sort your whole collection
• Import in bulk from folders or multi-page PDFs, pairing fronts and backs
• Auto-import from a watch folder on a schedule
• Track value over time, value by game, and set completion on a dashboard
• Edit any card and rotate/straighten its scans
• Export your collection to CSV anytime
• Personalize the app with team-color themes
• Adjustable text size, high-contrast mode, and keyboard navigation

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your collection lives in a local database on your PC. Lorebox has no accounts,
no analytics, and no cloud. Card images and names are sent to identification and
pricing services only when you use those features, using API keys you provide.

BRING YOUR OWN KEYS
Card identification uses the Anthropic (Claude) API, and pricing uses Scryfall
and the eBay API. You add your own free/low-cost API keys in Settings, so you're
always in control of usage and cost.

PLEASE NOTE
Estimated values and condition grades are automated and approximate. They are a
helpful guide for organizing and exploring your collection — not guaranteed sale
prices, professional appraisals, or a substitute for a professional grading
service.

Lorebox is open source (AGPL-3.0). Learn more at https://loreboxapp.dev
